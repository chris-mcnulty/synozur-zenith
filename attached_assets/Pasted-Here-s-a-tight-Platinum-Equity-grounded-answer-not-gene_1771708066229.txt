# The 3 MVP Features (Platinum‑Validated)

## 1️⃣ Governed Workspace Provisioning (Deal / PortCo Safe by Default)

**What it is**
A governed, self‑service **workspace creation flow** for:

*   Teams
*   SharePoint Team Sites
*   M365 Groups

Every workspace is created:

*   With **required metadata**
*   With a **sensitivity label**
*   With **default sharing rules**
*   With a **known lifecycle**

**Why this is MVP‑critical for Platinum**
Platinum’s engagement explicitly focuses on:

*   Deal and PortCo SharePoint sites as the **AI pilot testbed**
*   Preventing leakage between **PEA, Family Office, and Office of the Chair**
*   Establishing **go‑forward governance** for net‑new sites, not retroactive cleanup [\[SOW M365 I...- v3 Clean | Word\]](https://synozur.sharepoint.com/sites/Marketing/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B8A636C14-B0BD-407C-A839-47A689FF4746%7D&file=SOW%20M365%20IA%20Modernization%20-%20AI%20Readiness%20-%20v3%20Clean.docx&action=default&mobileredirect=true&DefaultItemOpen=1), [\[SOW M365 I...iness - v2 | Word\]](https://synozur.sharepoint.com/sites/LeadershipPrivateGroup/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B7CFB98E8-3778-4E26-8E9B-566DDFB33DD7%7D&file=SOW%20M365%20IA%20Modernization%20-%20AI%20Readiness%20-%20v2.docx&action=default&mobileredirect=true&DefaultItemOpen=1)

**What Zenith must do (MVP scope)**

*   Replace “free‑form site creation” with **policy‑driven requests**
*   Enforce:
    *   Naming standards (Deal / PortCo patterns)
    *   Ownership capture
    *   Sensitivity selection
*   Record a **governance system of record at creation time**

✅ This directly supports the AI pilot and reduces downstream risk.

## 2️⃣ Metadata + Sensitivity Label Enforcement for AI Readiness

**What it is**
Zenith ensures that **every governed workspace** has:

*   Required metadata (deal name, PortCo, confidentiality tier, steward)
*   A Microsoft Purview sensitivity label
*   Metadata structured to feed:
    *   Purview adaptive scopes
    *   Copilot inclusion/exclusion logic

**Why this is MVP‑critical for Platinum**
Platinum’s scope repeatedly emphasizes:

*   Content classification as the **foundation of AI readiness**
*   Using Purview + SharePoint/Syntex to make AI safe and auditable

**What Zenith must do (MVP scope)**

*   Validate required metadata at:
    *   Creation
    *   Key lifecycle checkpoints
*   Prevent “AI‑eligible but unlabeled” content
*   Show **metadata completeness** by workspace

✅ Without this, Copilot governance collapses.

## 3️⃣ Copilot Eligibility & Governance Explainability

**What it is**
A **clear, human‑readable answer** to the question:

> “Why *can* or *can’t* Copilot use this site?”

Based on:

*   Sensitivity label
*   Metadata
*   Policy decisions
*   Lifecycle state

**Why this is MVP‑critical for Platinum**
Platinum’s AI Governance Charter work stresses:

*   Human oversight
*   Explainability
*   Documented decision logic
*   SEC‑defensible controls around AI use [\[AI Governa...um Equity  | Word\]](https://synozur.sharepoint.com/sites/PlatinumEquity2/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7BC7CBFDA9-0A95-454B-BFD0-5B7253570C68%7D&file=AI%20Governance%20Charter%20Review%20%20Summary%20of%20Recommendations%20for%20Platinum%20Equity%20.docx&action=default&mobileredirect=true&DefaultItemOpen=1)

Platinum does **not** want:

*   Black‑box AI decisions
*   “Copilot is blocked, trust us” answers

**What Zenith must do (MVP scope)**

*   Show Copilot eligibility per workspace
*   Explain *which rule* caused inclusion or exclusion
*   Tie decisions back to:
    *   Policy
    *   Metadata
    *   Purview configuration (even if enforcement is external)

✅ This is essential for executive confidence and regulator comfort.
