Zenith
Microsoft 365 Governance, Provisioning & AI Control Plane
Complete Product Specification – v1.0

1. Product Vision
Zenith is the governance control plane for Microsoft 365.
It translates business intent into enforced, explainable, and auditable governance across Teams, SharePoint, Microsoft 365 Groups, Viva, Power Platform, and Copilot — without slowing users down or monitoring behavior.
Zenith is policy‑first, metadata‑driven, event‑based, and AI‑assisted, designed for enterprises operating across multiple tenants and environments.

2. Non‑Negotiable Design Principles
These are hard rules, not guidelines.
2.1 Governance, Not Monitoring
Zenith:
Governs creation, configuration, and lifecycle 
Explains policy outcomes 
Orchestrates Microsoft enforcement layers 
Zenith never:
Monitors performance 
Tracks user behavior 
Measures experience or health 
Performs observability or remediation 
(That boundary preserves Reveille.)

2.2 No Polling (Hard Constraint)

Zenith must never poll Microsoft 365 for state.
Allowed:
Event‑driven notifications 
Intent‑driven, transactional reads 
State captured at creation 
Enterprise batch analytics (MGDC) — explicit, optional, offline 
Forbidden:
Scheduled scans 
Background enumeration 
Drift crawlers 
“Check everything” jobs 

2.3 Purview Is the Enforcement Engine
Zenith configures and governs Purview.  Purview enforces.
Zenith does not re‑implement:
DLP 
Retention 
Label enforcement 
Records management 

2.4 Metadata Is the Control Surface
If it isn’t expressed as metadata, Zenith cannot govern it.

Tenant Governance Admin
